Dear UBA Partners:

Welcome to the 2015 Mid-Winter Meeting & Expo of United Benefit Advisors at the Hyatt Regency Coconut Point Resort & Spa in gorgeous Bonita Springs, Florida. It’s great to go somewhere warm, connect with many Principals, and continue learning from the shared wisdom of our collective organization.
